The are two different UIs provided here:

`phone` is used for:
- normal protrait phones.
- foldable phones with their screens folded.
- split view or float window view of tablets.
- PC, but for the users who want an experience of a mobile app (a bit weird lol).

`tablet` is used for:
- PC windows with a normal size (not too narrow).
- the full screen of tablets (either horizonal or protrait).
- foldable phones with their screens non folded.

Shared directory(ies) for them:

`colors`:                   Color scheme related data.
